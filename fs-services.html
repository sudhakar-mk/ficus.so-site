Consulting

Our team has extensive experience in the manufacturing industry, and we offer a range of consulting services to help you optimize your operations and achieve your business goals.

Whether you need help with compliance, quality control, or process optimization, we can provide the guidance and expertise you need to succeed. Implementation & Configuration

Our team of experts has extensive experience in implementing and configuring MES solutions for clients across a wide range of industries.

During the implementation phase, our team works closely with you to understand your business processes and identify the specific requirements of your organization. We then develop a deployment plan that is tailored to your unique needs, ensuring that the solution meets your specific needs and delivers maximum value.

Integration

We understand that your MES solution is just one piece of the puzzle, and we offer a range of integration services to ensure that your solution works seamlessly with your existing systems.

Our team has experience working with a variety of systems, including SCADA, LIMS, PLC and ERP solutions, and we can help you integrate your MES solution with your other systems to create a comprehensive solution that meets all of your needs.

Customization

We understand that every business is unique, and we offer a range of customization services to ensure that your MES solution meets your specific needs. Whether you need custom reports, user interfaces, or workflows, our team can develop solutions that are tailored to your business.

We will utilize AVEVA libraries to construct a template that can be employed to quickly and efficiently deploy multi-site solutions with minimal effort.

Maintainence & Training

Keep your AVEVA MES solution running smoothly with our comprehensive software maintenance services.

We handle updates, patches, performance monitoring, bug fixing, security enhancements, and provide user support and training. Focus on your core business while we ensure your AVEVA MES environment is secure, optimized, and reliable.
