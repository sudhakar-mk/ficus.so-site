Operations

Operational Excellence

MES streamlines the manufacturing process by automating manual processes, reducing paperwork, and enabling real-time communication between machines and operators.

MES allows for easy customization and adaptation to changing business needs, enabling manufacturers to adjust to changing market demands quickly.This leads to increased flexibility, improved responsiveness, and reduced costs.

KPI

OEE Tracking

MES provides real-time visibility into all aspects of the manufacturing process, enabling manufacturers to identify and address inefficiencies and bottlenecks quickly.By tracking KPIs, a factory can identify areas for improvement and take action to improve its overall performance.

This leads to improved productivity, increased throughput, and reduced downtime.

IIoT

Data Insights

IIoT platform that enables manufacturers to collect, store, and analyze data from their production processes and equipment. It helps manufacturers to perform predictive maintenance and avoid unplanned downtime.

It also streamlines data collection and analysis processes, provides a collaborative environment for sharing data and insights, and helps manufacturers make better-informed decisions.

Traceabilty

Streamline Manufacturing Data

MES enables manufacturers to track and trace raw materials, finished products, and production processes, enabling faster and more accurate recall and issue resolution.

MES provides real-time data and analytics on all aspects of the manufacturing process,enabling manufacturers to make data-driven decisions that optimize production and reduce waste.This leads to better visibility, increased agility, and improved decision-making.

Compliance

Quality and Standards

MES enables manufacturers to track and document compliance with regulatory requirements, such as FDA regulations, ISO standards, and GMP guidelines. This leads to improved compliance, reduced risk of non-compliance, and increased customer confidence.

MES allows manufacturers to identify & address quality issues quickly.This leads to enhanced product quality, increased customer satisfaction, and reduced costs associated with rework & scrap.

MDM

Model Driven MES

The approach to MES implementation and optimization that uses a model-based approach to design and implement the system.It is a powerful tool for optimizing and streamlining the manufacturing process.

By providing a virtual representation of the process, manufacturers can test different scenarios, optimize the process, and improve the accuracy and speed of data collection and analysis.
